About StarLight

This journey began as a creative class project. Initially, we explored building simulations, but our creative vision evolved when our friend Steff revealed their passionate world of writing. That's when we chose to build something magical - a space dedicated to storytelling.

We believe in the cosmic power of storytelling and the strength of shared experiences. Our platform serves as both observatory and launchpad for imagination. Here, every writer can connect, collaborate, and be inspired by fellow cosmic creators.
